02 — How we build them

We wind them.
We don't just sell them.

Drag the motor at the top to see the copper stator windings — that's the part we make in-house.

Stators wound in-house, aluminium-extrusion housings machined to size, pressure-die-cast end covers, branded FAG/SKF bearings and Class F/H insulation systems. Every motor is electrically tested before it leaves — built to the machine it runs on, not approximated from a catalogue.

03 — About

Built to spec,
not pulled from stock.

Most "motor suppliers" resell. We design, wind and test ours.

HD Electric manufactures textile spindle and winding motors, and custom special-purpose motors, for machine builders and mills. Tell us the application — the machine, load, speed, drive frequency, shaft and mounting — and we engineer, wind and test a motor that fits it.

Ring twisters, drum and TFO winders, or a one-off special — the work is the same: the right motor on the machine, wound properly, tested, and documented.
